Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Glintup ("we", "our", "us") is a daily learning app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

  • Phone number — Used for authentication via OTP verification. We do not share your phone number with third parties.
  • Reading preferences — Topics you select and cards you interact with, used to personalize your daily edition.
  • Usage data — Reading streaks, cards read, time spent — used to show you your progress stats.
  • Saved cards — Cards you bookmark are stored in your library.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or location
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not use tracking or advertising SDKs
  • We do not share data with third-party analytics

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security policies. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen in the app. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Glintup is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: privacy@glintup.app
