# Glintup - App Store Listings

## App Name
Glintup

## Subtitle (iOS) / Short Description (Android)
Your daily dose of learning. Then you're done.

## Description
Glintup delivers a curated daily edition of 10 beautifully designed learning cards — each one a bite-sized insight across science, history, psychology, technology, arts, business, nature, and space.

No endless scrolling. No algorithm chasing your attention. Just 10 cards, every day, personalized to your interests.

**HOW IT WORKS**
• Open the app → Your daily edition is ready
• Swipe through 10 cards (takes ~5 minutes)
• Done for the day. Come back tomorrow.

**7 CARD TYPES**
• Quick Facts — Surprising truths in one line
• Insights — Deep takeaways that change how you think
• Visual — Beautiful images with context
• Stories — Short narratives that stick
• Deep Reads — 3-minute explorations of fascinating topics
• Questions — Test what you've learned
• Quotes — Wisdom from great minds

**FEATURES**
• Personalized editions based on your topic preferences
• Reading streaks and daily progress tracking
• Save cards to your library for later
• Explore trending topics and deep dives
• Beautiful, calm design — no ads, no clutter

**YOUR TOPICS**
Choose from 8 topics: Science, History, Psychology, Technology, Arts & Culture, Business, Nature, and Space.
